# Debt Freedom Calculator - Business Plan

## Executive Summary

**Product**: Debt Freedom Calculator - A gamified, visual debt repayment planning tool
**Target Market**: US consumers with $1+ trillion in credit card debt
**Revenue Model**: One-time purchase ($29.99) + Affiliate commissions
**Projected MRR**: $5,000-$25,000 within 12 months

---

## üéØ Problem Statement

### The Market Pain
- **$1.13 trillion** in US credit card debt (2024)
- **Average household**: $7,000+ in credit card debt
- **Emotional burden**: Debt is the #1 source of financial stress
- **Information gap**: People don't know the best strategy to pay off debt
- **Motivation deficit**: Paying off debt feels overwhelming and endless

### Current Solutions (And Why They Fail)
| Solution | Problems |
|----------|----------|
| Spreadsheets | Not visual, no motivation, easy to abandon |
| Generic Calculators | No strategy comparison, boring, no tracking |
| Financial Advisors | Expensive ($100-300/hr), not accessible |
| Apps (Mint, etc.) | Passive tracking, no active payoff planning |

---

## üí° Solution

### Core Value Proposition
> "Know your exact debt-free date and save thousands in interest with a personalized, gamified payoff plan."

### Key Differentiators
1. **Visual Strategy Comparison** - Avalanche vs Snowball side-by-side
2. **Gamification** - Achievements, milestones, progress bars
3. **Real-time Recalculation** - See impact of extra payments instantly
4. **Zero Friction** - No signup, no data storage, instant results
5. **Affiliate Integration** - Monetizes at point of highest intent
